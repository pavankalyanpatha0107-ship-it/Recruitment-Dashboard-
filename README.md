# Recruitment-Dashboard-

## 📌 Overview

The **Recruitment Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze and visualize hiring trends, recruitment sources, employee distribution, and salary insights. It helps HR teams and decision-makers track key recruitment metrics efficiently.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 📅 **Hiring Trends Analysis** – Track employee hiring over the years
* 🌐 **Recruitment Source Insights** – Identify top-performing hiring platforms (Indeed, LinkedIn, etc.)
* 🧑‍💼 **Position-wise Performance** – Analyze performance scores across different job roles
* ⚖️ **Gender Distribution** – Visualize employee distribution by gender
* 📈 **Key Metrics Cards**

  * Total Employees
  * Total Recruitments
  * Number of Positions
  * Maximum Salary

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

* **Date of Hire Analysis**: Shows hiring patterns year-wise
* **Recruitment Sources**: Highlights the most effective hiring channels
* **Performance Score Distribution**: Helps understand employee performance across roles
* **Special Projects by Gender**: Displays participation split
* **Summary KPIs**: Quick snapshot of important metrics

---
